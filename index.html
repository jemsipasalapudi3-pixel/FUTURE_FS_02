About Us

Welcome to New Jerusalem International Bible Theological College, a premier institution dedicated to equipping faithful servants of God with sound theological education, spiritual maturity, and practical ministry skills. Located in Kovvuru (Andhra Pradesh, India), our college serves as a global hub for theological excellence, anchoring students in biblical truth and preparing them to impact nations for Christ. We offer comprehensive academic programs—ranging from Certificate and Diploma courses to Bachelor of Theology, Master of Divinity, Master of Theology, and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.)—designed to foster rigorous scholarship and a deep passion for the Gospel. Our Mission & Vision Our Vision: To be a globally recognized center of biblical learning that transforms lives, builds strong theological foundations, and sends empowered leaders into the global harvest field. Our Mission: To provide high-quality, accessible, and spirit-led theological training; to cultivate spiritual integrity and academic excellence; and to partner with international bodies to elevate the standards of ministry education in India and beyond.

Principal's Message

Welcome to our institution.

It is our privilege to partner with students who desire to deepen their understanding of God's Word and prepare for meaningful Christian service. Our commitment extends beyond academic instruction to the holistic development of every student through spiritual formation, mentorship, and ministry experience.

Whether you are preparing for pastoral ministry, missions, teaching, counseling, or leadership within the church, our programs are designed to equip you with biblical knowledge, theological understanding, and practical skills for effective service.

We invite you to become part of our learning community and join us in advancing God's mission throughout the world.
